About

The accidental part

I studied mathematics and computer science: training built around proofs, algorithms, and structure. Design wasn't part of the plan. It found me while I was building tools to make sense of data, and I never really left.

What I do now

These days I turn data into stories: interactive visualisations, dashboards, and small tools that make complex patterns legible to the people who need them: researchers, policymakers, and anyone curious enough to look. Recent work spans a scrollytelling data story built from every Indian Railways station in the country, a nutrition-reporting pipeline built for IFPRI's POSHAN initiative, and a community-driven look at India's traditional land measurement units.
